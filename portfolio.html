Oklahoma City University GPS System

As a senior capstone project, I challenge my knowledge and abilities to learn new skills. For example, I learned the language Kotlin. In a team environment, I was one of three people that created and designed a GPS app for the Oklahoma City University campus. When developing the idea for this capstone I knew there was a significant need for this application, especially for new students and transfer students as the campus was built in 1904 and is difficult to navigate due to many renovations throughout the years. This app was created by manually creating paths for the sidewalks as they were not mapped in Google Maps API.
